- DATABASE SERVER/CLUSTER: is the software platform that enables us to manipulate data inside RDBMS... it holds collection of DATABASES

- DATABASE: is COLLECTION of TABLES which hold DATA.

      ** the tables of 2 separate database can or cannot be related

- TABLE: is where data is stored in tabular form in ROWS(RECORDS/ENTRIES) and COLUMNS(FILDS)

- DATA: is the information stored in FIELDS (COLUMNS) of the table.

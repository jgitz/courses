- () - bracket has the highest operator precedence value of 3 

- *, /, %: multiplication, division and mod has the SECOND highest operator precedence of value 2 

- +, - : addition and subtraction has the least operator precedence value of 1 

** ASSOCIATIVITY: if 2 operator of same precedence value comes in an expression... the LEFT most operator has the HIGHEST PRIORITY and gets executed first....

** always use BRACKETS to group operands in the expression to get correct answers

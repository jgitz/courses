- char datatype is of integer datatype because alphabets and numbers are stored in the form of ASCII codes.

- ASCII codes for various characters are given below.... 

    - A = 65.. B = 66..... Z = 90

    - a = 97.. b = 98...... z = 122

    - 0 = 48... 1 = 49..... 9 = 57

      ** always know the  range of above.... other symbols also have ascii codes


** char datatype can take alphabets as well as ascii code numerals as value during a char variable assignment...

** char datatype can also be subjected to arithematic operations.

- short circuiting: is a method used with LOGICAL AND and OR operators to play with 2 conditons 

- AND SHORT CIRCUITING: 

    - syntax: 

        condition 1 && condition 2 

          ** if condition 1 is FALSE.... then condition 2 is NOT CHECKED 

          ** if condition 1 is TRUE... then only CONDITION 2 IS CHECKED

- OR SHORT CIRCUITING: 

    - syntax: 

        condition 1 || condition 2 

          ** if condition 1 is TRUE.... then condtion 2 is NOT CHECKED 

          ** if condition 1 is FALSE.... then conly CONDITION 2 IS CHECKED

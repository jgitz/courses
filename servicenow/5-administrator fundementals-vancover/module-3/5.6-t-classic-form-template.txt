- TEMPLATE: allows to AUTO POPULATE commonly used field values in FORMS.

  **** TEMPLATE CREATION SHOULD be restricted to SELECT GROUPS as it can BYPASS MANDATORY FIELDS, UI POLICIES etc.... specially on records with CONDITIONS and WORKFLOWS 

>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>

- to set template in CLASSIC FORM 

  - open FORM 

  - click on MORE OPTIONS (3 dots on top right)

  - click TOGGLE TEMPLATE BAR 

  - TEMPLATE BAR appears on the BOTTOM of the page 

  **** if there no templates are listed... you can click on CREATE TEMPLATE on the same bar

..... steps involved to make EMAIL NOTIFICATION from scratch 

- STEP 1: create EMAIL LAYOUT.... email layout allows you to create HTML CONTENT of the EMAI BODY.... it can be created in TWO WAYS..

  1) using an INLINE HTML EDITOR 

  2) MANUALLY ENTERING HTML CODE 

  ** to create EMAIL LAYOUT... goto

    - ALL > SYSTEM POLICY > EMAIL > LAYOUTS 

- STEP 2: create email TEMPLATE... to create templates... goto 

  - ALL > SYSTEM NOTIFICATION > EMAIL > TEMPLATES

  ** you APPLY EMAIL LAYOUT to TEMPLATES to FORMAT the BODY of the MESSAGES 


- STEP 3: create EMAIL NOTIFICATION by gointg to 

  - ALL > SYSTEM NOTIFICATION > EMAIL > NOTIFICATIONS

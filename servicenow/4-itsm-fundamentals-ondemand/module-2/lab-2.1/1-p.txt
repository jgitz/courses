..... to view order request from service catalog 

- log in as an employee user 

- goto SELF SERVICE > MY REQUESTS 

  ** all requests are listed here

  - click on a REQUEST to see the detils of the ORDER 

    - scroll down to the REQUESTED ITEM TAB to see the RITM records of the ordered items 

    - scroll down to the APPROVERS TAB to see the APPROVER who has to approve the request

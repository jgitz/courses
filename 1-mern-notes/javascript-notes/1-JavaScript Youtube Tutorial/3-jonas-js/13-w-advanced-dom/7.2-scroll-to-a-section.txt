.... to scroll to a section.... we have two methods 

- OLD METHOD; to scroll to a section use 

      window.scrollTo(<getBoundingClientRect_variable>.left + window.pageXOffset, <getBoundingClientRect_variable>.top + window.pageYOffset)


- to do smooth scrolling ; to smoothly scroll to a section

    window.scrollTo({
      left: <getBoundingClientRect_variable>.left + window.pageXOffsec

      top: getBoundingClientRect_variable>.top + window.pageYOffset

      behavior: "smooth"
    })

- NEW METHOD (works only on modern browsers): to scroll to a section using new method 

      <query_select_variable_section>.scrollIntoView({behavior: "smooth"})

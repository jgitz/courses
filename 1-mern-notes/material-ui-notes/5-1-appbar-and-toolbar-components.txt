- APPBAR component: is used to make a navigation bar in the mui

    - syntax:

        <AppBar>
        </AppBar>

          ** can add button, logo etc to build a navbar

          ** B is uppercase in AppBar tag

    - accepted propes 

        - variant: 

            - "elevation": gives shadows to the appbar.... this is dafault variant 

            - "outlined" : no shadow to app bar 

        - color 

- TOOLBAR component: provides a structure to provide sub elements in a navbar with automatic flex properties  

      - syntax: 

          <Toolbar>
          </Toolbar> 

          ** b is lowercase in toolbar tag

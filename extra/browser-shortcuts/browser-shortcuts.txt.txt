- select address bar url: ctl + L 

- search google via address bar: ctl + E....

	** to open it in new tab press: alt + enter

- back: alt + left arrow 

- front: alt + right arrow

- bookmark: ctl + D

- history: ctl + H

- scroll down: spacebar

- scroll up: shift + spacebar

- to see full source code: ctl + u

- to open delete search history menu: ctl + shift + delete

- to open browser menu: ctl + f

- to open new window: ctl + n

- to open new incognito window: ctl + shift + n

- to open new tab: ctl + t

- to open recently closed tab: ctl + shift + t

- to cycle between tabs: ctl + tab
